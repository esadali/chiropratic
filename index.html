Since 2002, Phillips Chiropractic has been serving Macon, Georgia, and the surrounding areas. We offer quality chiropractic care in a warm and welcoming environment. Our office takes pride in providing compassionate, individualized service with each patient as our first priority.

Dr. Phillips utilizes the most up-to-date chiropractic techniques in order to provide the best level of care possible. We treat neck, low back, shoulder, headaches, and many other types of joint and muscle pain caused by injuries and the stress of everyday life. Pain and other symptoms are indications that you should see a doctor of chiropractic who deals with the nervous system and spine. With our techniques and exercises, we can help you relieve these aches and pains.
